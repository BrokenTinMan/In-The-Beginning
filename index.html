The Montross Mother

In The Beginning

Montross Virgina is a quiet town. Known for it's thriving community of In the summer of 1934, Mildred Fanypants (A.K.A. The Montross Mother) was seen taking three young children into the woods.
Witnesses say they saw Fanypants emerge from the woods later that day.
The children were found 2 weeks later, half starved and not all there.
Fanypants was taken into custody but released after it was discovered that the children were actually small adults.
When asked why she had taken them into the woods, Fanypants told authorities "The migdets were creeping me out so I had them follow me into the woods with promises of candy and stilts"
"My plan was to take them to the tall weeds and leave them there in hopes they would get lost".
When asked why, she replyed "Half-stacks are not normal and should be left in the woods".
